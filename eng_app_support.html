Notre Dame Transfer Application Checklist

Each student will apply to transfer to Notre Dame in the Spring semester of their forth year

  • January 17: Dual Degree Application made avaliable
  • February 1:
    • Dual Degree Engineering Application - Filled out by Student
    • College Transcript - Provided by Advisor
    • High School transcript - Student must reach out to their high school
    • Letter of Recommendation - Provided by Advisor
    • Dual Degree Engineering College Offical Report - Provided by Advisor
    • International Financial Documentation (for international students only)
  • February 10: Financial Aid documents due
    • FAFSA
    • CSS Profile
    • Tax documents

Transfer Life

Every student brings a unique journey to Notre Dame, and transferring is no exception. Building a strong sense of community and making the most of available resources—both before and after transferring—is key to a smooth and enriching transition.

One of the best ways to integrate into campus life is by getting involved early. As a Saint Mary’s student planning to transfer, you can already participate in many of Notre Dame’s clubs and organizations. These groups offer not only a chance to explore your interests but also to build friendships, mentorships, and professional networks.

  • Academic and Professional Clubs:
    • Society of Women Engineers (SWE) – Joint chapter with ND and SMC
    • Irish SAT
    • Robotic Football Team
    • Rocketry Club
    • CS Club
  • Arts, Media, and Campus Life:
    • Ballroom Dance Club
    • The Observer (ND-SMC joint student newspaper)
    • Notre Dame Marching Band – Open to Saint Mary’s students

Assigned Advisor Directory

Each student is assigned a primary advisor from either Saint Mary’s College or Notre Dame depending on their current year in the program.

  • Director: Kristin Kuter (kjehring@saintmarys.edu)
  • First-Year Students: Meet with your CAM program advisor for general curriculum guidance.
  • Second-Year Students: Begin planning your engineering course requirements and Notre Dame course sequencing.
  • Third-Year and Beyond: Connect with Notre Dame CS advisors and internship coordinators.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which courses transfer to Notre Dame?
Use the program guide and work with your advisor to review credit equivalencies and transfer eligibility each semester.

What GPA is required to remain in the dual degree program?
You must maintain a cumulative GPA of 3.3 or higher and pass all required core courses.

When should I submit my Notre Dame application?
By Spring semester of your fourth year. Advisors will assist you in preparing transcripts and forms.

Can I take summer courses?
Yes. Summer coursework at either institution may help lighten your regular semester load, but must be approved in advance.

Who do I contact if I’m unsure about my schedule?
Your assigned advisor is your first point of contact. You can also reach out to Dr. Kristin Kuter for program-wide questions.

Degree Requirements for CAM and CS

The dual degree program includes a Bachelor of Science in Computing and Applied Mathematics (CAM) from Saint Mary's College and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Notre Dame.

  • Minimum 120 credits for the CAM degree, including core curriculum and major requirements.
  • Computer Science major at Notre Dame includes courses in systems, algorithms, programming languages, and software engineering.
  • Students must maintain a GPA of 3.3 or higher to remain in good standing for transfer.

Prerequisites & Engineering Transfer Requirements

Students must complete foundational STEM courses before transferring into Notre Dame’s CS program:

  • Completion of Calculus I & II, General Physics I & II with labs
  • At least one programming course using a structured language (e.g., C, Java, Python)
  • Minimum 3.3 cumulative GPA at Saint Mary's
  • Official transcript and application form submitted by Spring of the third year
  • Academic advisor and program director approval required

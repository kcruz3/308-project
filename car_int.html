Career Development and Handshake Access

At Saint Mary’s College, the Career Crossings Office is dedicated to helping students explore career paths, prepare job materials, and connect with professional opportunities. They offer one-on-one advising, résumé and cover letter reviews, mock interviews, and workshops tailored to students at all stages of their academic journey. You’ll also find help applying to grad school, attending career fairs, and networking with alumnae. Both Saint Mary’s and Notre Dame use Handshake:
  • Notre Dame Handshake
  • Saint Mary’s Handshake
